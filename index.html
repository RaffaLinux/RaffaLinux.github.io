Interpretability and Complexity Reduction in IoT Network Anomaly Detection Via XAI

This work studies the use of XAI to reduce the complexity of Deep Learning based Anomaly-Based Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS) by removing unimportant features. In particular, it uses the Kitsune and IoT23 datasets made of traffic from infected IoT devices belonging to botnets.
